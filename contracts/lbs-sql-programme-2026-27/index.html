Between Avid Learning and London Business School
SQL for Business Leaders — 2026/27 Academic Year
Supplier Avid Learning, sole trader (trading name), Courtney Laing, United Kingdom
Client London Business School, Regent's Park, London NW1 4SA
Effective date On signature by both parties
Term From the Effective Date to 31 August 2027 (the 2026/27 academic year)
Total fee £28,500 + VAT if applicable (see clause 4)

1.Background

Avid Learning has previously delivered SQL workshops to LBS MBA cohorts, including a paid trial on 24 April 2026 attended by 11 students, with positive feedback and demonstrable demand. This Statement of Work (SOW) sets out the terms on which Avid Learning will deliver three (3) cohort runs of the SQL for Business Leaders programme to LBS during the 2026/27 academic year.

This SOW is intended to be light-touch and pragmatic. If LBS requires Avid Learning to contract under LBS's standard supplier terms, the parties will substitute those terms in good faith, retaining the commercial provisions in clauses 2–5.

2.Scope of services

Avid Learning will deliver three (3) cohort runs of the SQL for Business Leaders programme. Each cohort run consists of two workshops:

  • Part 1 — SQL: Foundations (3 hours)
  • Part 2 — SQL: AI & Applications (3 hours)

Each cohort accommodates up to 25 students. For each cohort, Avid Learning will provide:

  • Live workshop delivery (in-person at LBS or online, at LBS's election)
  • Participant handout (PDF)
  • Access to a hosted SQL practice environment for 6 months from the date of Part 1
  • A post-cohort feedback report including NPS, satisfaction scores and qualitative comments

Founding Partner status

LBS is recognised as Avid Learning's Founding Partner for the 2026/27 academic year. This includes:

  • Use of the LBS–Avid Learning relationship in marketing, subject to LBS's prior written approval of any specific use of the LBS name or logo
  • A 5% discount applied to the standard per-cohort price (already reflected in the fees in clause 4)
  • Extended access to the SQL practice environment for all students (an additional 6 months beyond the initial 6 months)

3.Schedule and delivery

The three cohorts will be scheduled across the 2026/27 academic year as follows:

Cohort Workshop Indicative window Format
1 SQL: Foundations + SQL: AI & Applications Autumn term 2026 In-person at LBS or online (LBS to choose)
2 SQL: Foundations + SQL: AI & Applications Spring term 2027 In-person at LBS or online (LBS to choose)
3 SQL: Foundations + SQL: AI & Applications Summer term 2027 (pre-internship) In-person at LBS or online (LBS to choose)

Specific dates for each cohort will be agreed by the parties in writing (email is sufficient) at least 30 days before the proposed delivery date. Avid Learning will use reasonable endeavours to accommodate LBS's preferred dates but is not obligated to deliver on dates that materially conflict with prior commitments.

If LBS cancels a confirmed cohort:

  • More than 30 days before the scheduled date: no fee
  • Between 14 and 30 days before: 50% of the per-cohort fee (£4,750)
  • Less than 14 days before: 100% of the per-cohort fee (£9,500)

If Avid Learning is unable to deliver a confirmed cohort due to illness or other reasonable cause, Avid Learning will offer a substitute date within 60 days at no additional cost. If no substitute date can be agreed, the per-cohort fee will not be charged.

4.Fees and payment

The total fee for the three cohort runs is £28,500 (twenty-eight thousand five hundred pounds sterling), payable in three instalments of £9,500 as set out below:

Milestone Trigger Amount
Mobilisation Within 14 days of signature £9,500
Cohort 2 delivery Within 14 days of completion of Cohort 2 £9,500
Cohort 3 delivery Within 14 days of completion of Cohort 3 £9,500
Total £28,500

All fees are exclusive of VAT. Avid Learning is currently below the UK VAT registration threshold and will not charge VAT unless and until registration is required, in which case VAT will be added at the prevailing rate.

Invoices are payable within 30 days of issue.

5.Intellectual property

All course materials — including slides, exercises, datasets, the practice environment, and any derivative works — remain the property of Avid Learning. LBS and its students are granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the materials for educational purposes during the term and for 12 months thereafter.

LBS may not redistribute, sublicence, or use the materials to deliver competing training, whether internally or via a third party, without Avid Learning's prior written consent.

Feedback, testimonials and aggregated, anonymised performance data (e.g. NPS scores, satisfaction ratings) may be used by Avid Learning for marketing purposes. Identifiable student data will not be shared without consent.

6.Confidentiality and data

Each party will keep confidential any non-public information disclosed by the other in connection with this SOW. This obligation survives termination for two (2) years.

Avid Learning will process student personal data (name, email, attendance, feedback responses) solely for the purpose of delivering the workshops and producing the feedback report. Avid Learning acts as a data processor on LBS's behalf and will comply with UK GDPR. Student data will be deleted within 90 days of the end of the term unless LBS instructs otherwise in writing.

7.Liability

Neither party excludes liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud, or any other liability that cannot be limited by law.

Subject to the above, each party's total aggregate liability under this SOW is capped at the total fees paid or payable under it (£28,500). Neither party is liable for indirect, consequential, or loss-of-profit damages.

Avid Learning maintains professional indemnity and public liability insurance appropriate to the services and will provide evidence on request.

8.Termination

Either party may terminate this SOW for material breach by the other if the breach is not remedied within 30 days of written notice.

LBS may terminate for convenience on 60 days' written notice. In that event, LBS will pay for any cohorts already delivered and 50% of the fee for any cohort confirmed but not yet delivered.

9.General

This SOW is governed by the laws of England and Wales and the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.

This SOW constitutes the entire agreement between the parties in respect of its subject matter. Variations must be agreed in writing and signed by both parties.

Neither party may assign this SOW without the other's prior written consent.
