The opportunity

LBS students are entering a business environment where data literacy is no longer optional. SQL is the most widely used data tool in finance, consulting, product, and operations, and demand for it among LBS students is demonstrably high.

Before LBS engaged formally, 20 students sought out and paid for this content independently, signalling an unmet need within LBS.

What the first cohorts showed

The inaugural LBS cohorts delivered the metrics above across 13 student respondents. 20 students have joined a waiting list for the Intermediate SQL course.

Why this partnership makes sense
  • LBS alumna — direct understanding of what LBS students need to be commercially competitive
  • Data scientist by training — practical, employer-relevant content grounded in real-world use
  • Qualified teacher — structured pedagogy, not ad hoc instruction
  • AI-literate — curriculum that reflects the tools students will work alongside from day one
